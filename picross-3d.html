Picross 3D Recreation

A recreation of picross 3D, with GPU instancing for the cubes, allowing 3 drawcalls for up to a 10x10x10 grid of cubes, each with their own marked color, base color, number per axis etc. The levels are saved and loaded as json, allowing easy sharing, and can be created and customised through a built in level editor.
